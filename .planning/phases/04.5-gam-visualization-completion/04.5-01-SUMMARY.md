- Removed `map_x_ex` / `map_y_ex` helper functions from `gam_plot.rs`; replaced with `axes.plot_bounds()` + inline arithmetic

## Decisions Made

- Accepted deliberate duplication between `plot_bounds()` and `to_primitives()` Steps 1-2 (research Option a) — refactoring `to_primitives()` to call `plot_bounds()` internally would require introducing a temporary allocation in a hot rendering path and risk introducing regressions
- `plot_bounds()` is data-space only — returning pixel coordinates would couple the method to canvas layout, breaking encapsulation
